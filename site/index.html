Transformation Coaching & Consulting LLC is a human resource consultancy with a primary focus on executive coaching and leadership development. Barbara works with her clients to support them in becoming more effective and impactful leaders, who achieve outcomes of enduring value. They are from a wide variety of industries and occupations, and are from organizations that range in size from smaller professional services firms to the Fortune 100.

Barbara draws on over 30 years of professional experience in multiple industries and disciplines. Her skills in coaching, organizational development, human resources, meeting design and facilitation, and business are broad and deep.

Prior to moving to consulting she was a Vice President of Human Resources, North America, for Dun & Bradstreet, and served as the primary architect of a 12 month cohort based comprehensive Leadership Development Program.

She spent eight years in the pharmaceutical industry in several roles, including Vice President of Human Resources, North America, for Pfizer Consumer Healthcare, where she was a member of the executive team. She also served as a Senior Director on the Worldwide Organizational Effectiveness and Consulting Services team for Pfizer Global Pharmaceuticals.

Prior to that she was with Prudential Healthcare where she was responsible for Diversity and Inclusion, EEOC Reporting and Planning, and Executive Recruiting. She was their Chief Financial Officer of Group Systems before transitioning into human resources.

In addition, her finance career included serving as Chief Financial Officer of Teach for America, multiple finance and accounting roles with Digital Equipment Corporation, and as an auditor with KPMG.

Barbara has a MS in Organizational Development from American University, received a BA in Accounting from Rutgers University, and is an inactive CPA who was previously licensed by the State of New Jersey.
